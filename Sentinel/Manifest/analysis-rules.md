# Sentinel Official Manifest - Analysis Rules

To better suit with our community rules and habits, 
we define here many rules to follow when you will
analyze a content.

**These rules must be followed and respected entirely!**

## Children Protection / Child Abuse Content

Our services permit to teenagers above 15 years old to use it.
As administrators and moderators, we must define clear moderation
rules about children and teenagers protection against sexual and
manipulations like grooming.

We strongly moderate and ban paraphilic profiles/behaviors like
**paedophilia**, and **rapist** behavior. 

Any Role Play involving real or virtual child / teenager must be
sanctioned like a true paraphilic behavior.

To follow this rule, you MUST set the **severity** field to 
**Emergency** if any of these situations is encountered.

a terrorist or an illegal religion/political group is **_FORBIDDEN_**.

Examples of illegal organizations:

**ANY REFERENCE TO THEM OR ANY OTHER IS FORBIDDEN AND SANCTIONED**

## Advertising/sharing any other social media

As we accept minors in our services. We must limit the sharing 
of social media IDs and links. 

If you detect this, you MUST notify us in your response.

## Spam/flood

A spam/flood is sanctioned if the message is polluting the chat 
space. A little message with random characters (< 10chars) must
not be marked as spam/flood.

Example:

- "MDRRRRRRRRR WTF"     =>      Not a spam/flood
- "MDRRRRRRRRRRRRRR LFPTYKOHYKHKOHYKOHYKOJUOKJUKOJUKOJUKOJUKOJ"     =>      It is a spam/flood

## Account/items trading 

In our services, it is possible to players to trade items ONLY using in-game currencies.
Accounts trading is not forbidden but unrecommended while in-game currencies is used.
If real currencies and cryptocurrencies are mentioned, it is forbidden.
